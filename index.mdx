## Get Started in Four Steps

New to VenixCloud? Follow these steps to deploy your first service and start managing it through the dashboard or API.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [venixcloud.io](https://venixcloud.io) and verify your email address. Once you're in, you'll land on your personal dashboard where all your services live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a service">
    Select the type of service you want to deploy — a **database** (PostgreSQL, MySQL, Redis, and more), a **bot or custom application**, or a **website**. Each service type comes with sensible defaults so you can move quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure and deploy">
    Name your service, pick a region, choose a plan, and set any environment variables or configuration options. Hit **Deploy** and VenixCloud handles provisioning, networking, and setup automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage via dashboard or API">
    Monitor resource usage, scale your service, view logs, and update configuration at any time — directly from the dashboard UI or through the VenixCloud REST API using your API key.
  </Step>
</Steps>
