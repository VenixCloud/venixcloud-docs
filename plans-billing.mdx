VenixCloud is built on the belief that powerful cloud infrastructure should be accessible at every stage of a project — from a weekend prototype to a production application serving thousands of users. That's why every plan, including the free tier, gives you real resources and a genuine path to scale. You only pay for what you need, and you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.

## Available plans

The table below compares all four VenixCloud plans so you can choose the one that best fits your workload and budget.

| Free       | R$0/mo     | 1         | 256 MB  | Shared     | 1 GB     | Community   |
| Starter    | R$29/mo    | 5         | 512 MB  | 1 vCPU     | 10 GB    | Email       |

  Resource limits listed above apply **per service**. For example, on the Pro plan, each of your 20 services can use up to 2 GB of RAM independently.
</Note>

## Billing cycle

VenixCloud bills on a **monthly cycle**. Your billing period starts on the date you first subscribe to a paid plan, and renews on that same calendar date each month — for example, if you subscribe on the 14th, you'll be charged on the 14th of every subsequent month. You can view your next billing date and a full invoice history at any time in **Settings → Billing**.

## Payment methods

VenixCloud supports the following payment methods:

- **Credit card** — Visa, Mastercard, and American Express are accepted. Your card is charged automatically at the start of each billing cycle.
- **PIX** — Available for customers in Brazil. Generate a PIX QR code or copy key directly from the billing page and complete payment within 30 minutes.
- **Boleto** — Also available for Brazilian customers. Boletos are issued up to 3 business days before your renewal date and expire after 3 business days. Late payment will pause your services until the boleto is confirmed.

To update your payment method, go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods** and click **Add Payment Method**.

## Upgrading your plan

You can upgrade to a higher plan at any time without any downtime to your existing services.

1. Open the dashboard and navigate to **Settings → Plan**.
2. Click **Upgrade Plan** and select the plan you want to move to.
3. Review the prorated charge for the remainder of your current billing cycle, then confirm the upgrade.

Your new resource limits take effect immediately after the upgrade is confirmed. Existing services are automatically updated to reflect the expanded CPU, RAM, and storage allocations of your new plan.

## Cancelling your subscription

To cancel your VenixCloud subscription, go to **Settings → Plan** and click **Cancel Subscription** at the bottom of the page. You'll be asked to confirm your decision and optionally provide feedback.

After cancellation, your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period — you won't be charged again, and your services will continue running until that date. Once the billing period expires, your account is downgraded to the Free plan. If you have more services than the Free plan allows, any services beyond the first will be stopped and scheduled for deletion.

<Warning>
  **Your data will be permanently deleted 7 days after a service is stopped due to a plan downgrade or cancellation.** This action is irreversible. Make sure to export any databases, logs, or files you need before your billing period ends. VenixCloud is not responsible for data loss resulting from account cancellation or non-payment.
</Warning>
